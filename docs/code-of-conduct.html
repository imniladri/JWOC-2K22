Code of Conduct

Read the code of conduct carefully!

As a part of the JWoC team and community we will be working with each other for more than a month on various open-source projects, so there will be a huge diversity of skills, experiences and opinions. We value each of your individual opinion but we would value humble behavior more. Humble behavior includes:

  • Be considerate and try to keep a considerate and helpful mentality because it is the main working principle of any community.
  • Be respectful to others. At some point of time, you may not agree with others that is totally fine, but disagreement should not lead to any personal attack or poor behavior.
  • Be careful with the words you choose, there are no such strict rules but we expect some level of professionals and you are an adult, you know what not to say.
