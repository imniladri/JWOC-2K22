JGEC WINTER OF CODE

JWoC (JGEC Winter of Code) is a coding event organised by JGEC which helps students to plunge into Open Source contribution and get the realm of Software Development. In association with

Google Developer Student Club, JGEC Jalpaiguri Govt. Engg. College

About Us

JWoC: How it Works?

Participants (restrained to 1st and 2nd year undergraduate students) participate for the program. Mentors willing to Open Source their projects in JWoC submit their proposals. The best projects are selected for this program. Students get acquainted about the projects from the mentors during the Community Bonding Period. Students work on these projects during the coding phase. At the end of the coding period, the winners of the programs are announced on the basis of their contribution in terms of quantity as well as quality.
